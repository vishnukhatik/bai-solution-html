Atishe Chordia

Co-Founder & CEO

Atishe is a serial entrepreneur at heart and loves building product experiences.

Nishyta Chordia

Co-Founder & CDO

Nishyta finished up her undergrad in Art History & Design and graduated with a Masters in Design from the the University of the Arts London. She leads design thinking workshops for all products at doodleblue and her passions include UX, art history & illustration.

Hemant Chordia

Director

Tripti Chordia

Human Resource

Tripti completed her masters in Advertising and Marketing from the UK. She has experience in marketing & strategy roles for leading media conglomerates. At doodleblue she leads resource growth which involves training & growing a highly specialised set of engineers & creative professionals.
